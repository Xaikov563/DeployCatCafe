About Cup of Cats

The Purrfect Blend of Coffee and Companionship!

Our Mission

To create a harmonious space where coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts can find joy, comfort, and connection while supporting cat welfare in our community.

Our Vision

To be the leading cat café that sets the standard for animal welfare, coffee excellence, and community engagement, inspiring similar initiatives worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 1970, Cup of Cats began as a dream to combine two passions: exceptional coffee and cat welfare. What started as a small café with three rescue cats has grown into a beloved community hub, having helped over 500 cats find forever homes.

Our journey has been one of love, dedication, and countless purrs of approval from both our feline friends and satisfied customers.

Artisanal Coffee

Locally roasted, ethically sourced beans prepared by skilled baristas for the perfect cup every time.

Friendly Cats

Meet our loving rescue cats, each with their own unique personality and story to share.

Fresh Pastries

Handcrafted daily pastries that perfectly complement our coffee selection.

Community Impact

Through our adoption program and community events, we've made a significant impact:

  • 500+ cats adopted
  • $50,000+ raised for local shelters
  • 1000+ volunteer hours contributed
  • Weekly educational programs on cat care

A Message from Our Founder
